Academics at SUNY New Paltz

Explore our diverse range of academic programs and find the right fit for you.

We offer over 100 undergraduate majors and more than 50 graduate programs across various disciplines, including arts, sciences, business, education, and engineering. Our faculty are dedicated to providing personalized instruction and mentorship to help you achieve your academic and career goals.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, research opportunities, and experiential learning programs ensure that you gain the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in today's competitive job market.
