About SUNY New Paltz

SUNY New Paltz is a public university in New Paltz, New York. We offer a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs.

Founded in 1828, SUNY New Paltz has a rich history of academic excellence and community engagement. Our campus is home to over 8,000 students from diverse backgrounds, fostering a vibrant and inclusive learning environment.

Our mission is to provide high-quality education that prepares students for successful careers and meaningful lives. We are committed to academic innovation, sustainability, and social responsibility.
